pedro the penguin

pedro looks innocent, but has a dark past. born in one of the roughest neighbourhoods of south antarctica, he has managed to attend zoom university despite the temptations of crime and vice.

pedro is played by pingüino paddler, 7-time tony award winner.

quacky the rubber duck

quacky is the son of scrooge mcduck, one of the richest ducks in the city. raised in a life of comfort, quacky often finds himself in trouble because of his extravagant tastes.

quacky is played by canard dubois, a duck we found in the forest.

teddy the bear

teddy is the best student of zoom university. capable of doing anything to raise her grades, teddy is the teacher's pet. her strategy got her through high school, but will it work in college?

teddy is played by arkouda thalassa, 2-time oscar nominee.

kimi the ice bear

kimi is a recently arrived exchange student from finland, who somehow has seen the other students in his feverish childhood nightmares. kimi has a psychic connection across zoom to all the other students, but will he use his powers for good?

kimi is played by kimi räikonnen, 2007 formula one world champion.
