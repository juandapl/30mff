synopsiS

in an online university class, enemies-turned-classmates quacky, teddy, pedro, and kimi find each other staring into an even scarier foe: the zoom screen. their shared history gives rise to distrust, hostility, and contempt. When they realise their common enemy is more important than their differences, they also notice it has already taken over their lives.

the four are forced by the circumstances to forgive the grievances of the past, and help each other prepare for the ultimate face-off. however, despite their newfound friendship, each one will have to fight their personal battle against zoom alone.
